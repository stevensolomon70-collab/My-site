Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 5, 2026

Fantle ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to provide the Fantle experience:

  • Game statistics — Your wins, streaks, guess distributions, and skill tier progress. This is stored locally on your device and optionally synced via iCloud.
  • Subscription status — Whether you are a Free or Fantle MVP subscriber, managed through Apple's App Store.
  • Game Center data — If you opt into Game Center, your display name and scores may appear on leaderboards.
  • Advertising data — Free users see ads served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve personalized ads, subject to your tracking preferences.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the Fantle game experience
  • To sync your progress across devices via iCloud
  • To display leaderboards via Apple Game Center
  • To serve advertisements to free users via Google AdMob
  • To process subscription purchases via Apple's App Store

Data Storage

Your game data is stored locally on your device using iOS standard storage (UserDefaults). If you are signed into iCloud, your stats may be synced across your Apple devices using CloudKit. We do not operate our own servers or databases.

Third-Party Services

Fantle uses the following third-party services:

  • Apple App Store — For subscription purchases and payments
  • Apple Game Center — For leaderboards (optional)
  • Apple CloudKit — For iCloud sync (optional)
  • Google AdMob — For serving ads to free users

Each of these services has their own privacy policy. We encourage you to review them.

Advertising & Tracking

Free users see banner and interstitial ads served by Google AdMob. When you first launch Fantle, you will be asked for permission to track your activity for personalized ads in accordance with Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework. You can change this preference at any time in your iPhone Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

Fantle MVP subscribers do not see any advertisements.

Children's Privacy

Fantle is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your Rights

You can reset or delete your game data at any time by deleting the app from your device. To opt out of personalized advertising, go to iPhone Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking and disable tracking for Fantle.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the date at the top of this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@fantle.app.
